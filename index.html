Services

What We Do

A focused set of services built around what service and industrial businesses actually need.

Website Development

We design and build professional websites from scratch — structured around your services, service areas, and the customers you want to reach. Every site is mobile-first, fast-loading, and built with conversion in mind.

  • Custom multi-page design
  • Mobile-responsive layouts
  • Contact and quote forms
  • Service area pages
  • Photo galleries and project showcases
  • Google Maps and location integration

Website Maintenance

Websites need care over time. We offer ongoing maintenance plans so your site stays updated, secure, and working correctly without you having to manage it yourself.

  • Content and text updates
  • Photo and media replacements
  • Technical updates and security patches
  • Form and function testing
  • Monthly performance reports

Hosting & Deployment

We handle the full technical stack — domain setup, SSL certificate, hosting configuration, and deployment. No technical knowledge required on your end.

  • Domain registration and transfer
  • SSL certificate (HTTPS)
  • Managed hosting setup
  • Uptime monitoring
  • Backup and recovery

SEO Basics

Local and organic search visibility starts with the fundamentals. We set up your site to be found by the right people in your service area.

  • Keyword-targeted page structure
  • Meta titles and descriptions
  • Google Search Console setup
  • Local schema markup
  • Image optimization
  • Site speed improvements

Digital Presence Management

Your website is one piece of your online presence. We help make sure the rest of it — Google Business, directories, social profiles — matches your brand and is working for you.

  • Google Business Profile setup and optimization
  • Directory listing submissions
  • NAP consistency (name, address, phone)
  • Review generation strategy
  • Brand consistency audit
